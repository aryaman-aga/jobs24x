Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

⚠️ Important: This is a Hobby Project

Jobs24X7 is a vibe coded hobby project built for fun. It is not a real business and does not have enterprise-grade data protection. Do not share sensitive personal information on this platform. We collect minimal data and make no guarantees about data security or privacy. Use at your own risk.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary for the App to function:

  • Account data: Email address and password (hashed and stored securely) when you create an account
  • Profile preferences: Job categories, experience level, and preferred location that you voluntarily provide
  • Usage data: Pages visited, jobs viewed/saved, and hackathon visits to improve your experience
  • Payment data: When you subscribe, payment processing is handled entirely by Razorpay — we do not see or store your full payment details
  • Session data: Basic session information to keep you logged in

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as government IDs, financial account numbers, medical information, or biometric data. Please do not share such information through the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality (job listings, hackathon listings)
  • To personalize job recommendations based on your stated preferences
  • To send job alerts that you have explicitly requested
  • To communicate with you about your account, subscription status, and important updates
  • To improve the App based on usage patterns and feedback
  • To prevent abuse, fraud, and violations of our Terms of Service

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data for advertising or marketing purposes. However, please be aware:

  • Razorpay: Payment processing is handled by Razorpay. Your payment data is subject to Razorpay's privacy policy
  • Google OAuth: If you sign up with Google, we receive only the email address and name you authorize
  • Hosting: The App is hosted on Render (for now) — data is stored on their servers
  • Analytics: We may use basic anonymized analytics to understand usage patterns
  • No advertising networks: We do not share data with advertisers or ad networks

We may disclose your information if required by law, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or prevent harm.

4. Data Security

We implement basic security measures including password hashing, HTTPS encryption, and session management. However, this is a hobby project and we provide no guarantees of data security.

  • Passwords are hashed using Django's built-in password hashing
  • All traffic is encrypted via HTTPS
  • Database access is restricted and credentials are stored as environment variables
  • We do not have dedicated security personnel or regular security audits
  • The App may have vulnerabilities inherent in any software project

You use this App at your own risk. We recommend:

  • Using a unique password that you do not use on other services
  • Not sharing sensitive personal information through the platform
  • Not relying on this App for critical or sensitive activities

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes (e.g., payment records may be retained for tax purposes).

Usage data and logs may be retained for analytics and debugging purposes and are periodically purged. We do not guarantee immediate deletion of all data upon account deletion.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the contact form on the App. Since this is a hobby project, please allow reasonable time for a response.

7. Cookies & Tracking

The App uses essential cookies for functionality:

  • Session cookie: To keep you logged in during your visit
  • CSRF cookie: For security against cross-site request forgery
  • Referral cookie: To track affiliate referrals (30-day duration)

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. We do not use fingerprinting or any other covert tracking techniques.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

9. Third-Party Links

The App contains links to external websites (job listings, company career pages, hackathon platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the App after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Since this is a hobby project, we don't have a formal privacy team. If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out through the contact form on the App.

Summary

We collect minimal data (email, preferences, usage). We don't sell your data. We use basic security but make no guarantees since this is a hobby project. Don't share sensitive info. Use at your own risk.
